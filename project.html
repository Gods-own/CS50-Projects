History: Remember that one of the things Idumeka loves doing is learning languages. She is currently learning French, Yoruba and German. The limitation she faced is what she doesn't want other people who want to learn the language to face so she has taken it upon herself to develop an application for learning Nigerian languages. She believes Nigeria being a culturally diverse country, aside from fostering unity, the app will be of tremendous benefit in other areas.

Stage: She is currently at the stage of making sentences, when she is done is will give a Yoruba expert to translate does sentences
