Biography

Idumeka Oritogun studied Petroleum Engineering in the University of Ibadan but she is currently learning web development on her own because that is where her interest lies. As at the time she decided to study petroleum engineering, she actually didn't know what to study but she just had to pick something so she opted for petroleum engineering. She started learning web development in March 2020 during the COVID-19 lockdown. She is really grateful to God for leading her on this path. Life has not really been rosy but even through life's ups and downs, God saw her through it all. She hopes one day she will be able to tell the story of her life and inspire people.
